COCONUT SUGAR


- Imported from Indonesia -
Also known as coconut palm sugar, is made from sap of a coconut palm that has been extracted and then boiled and dehydrated. Low on fructose and is known to be the healthiest sugar yet.

HALF AND HALF


- Manufactured in our Factory -
Although we call it "Half and Half," the more accurate way to say it is "Three Fourth and One Fourth." The reason that is because we put much more cream than just milk to ensure that you get that rich, creaminess with your cup of coffee.

DARK BROWN SUGAR


- Manufactured in our Factory -
Brown sugar is made by refining white sugar and adding molasses. Our light brown sugar contains about 6.5% molasses and has a more complex flavor, which characterize as similar to caramel or toffee.

LIGHT BROWN SUGAR


- Manufactured in our Factory -
Brown sugar is made by refining white sugar and adding molasses. Our light brown sugar contains about 3.5% molasses and is naturally moist and soft because of the thin film of molasses that covers each sugar crystal.

RAW HONEY


- Imported from New Zealan -
The raw is R.E.A.L. 100% Pure, unadulterated honey from the highest quality bee keeper in New Zealand. Unprocessed, we send it right to you!

SALTED CARAMEL


- Manufactured in our Factory -
Salty and sweet. The perfect combonation. Inspired by French culinary, we've created our own "melt in your mouth" salted caramel to enhance and add more flavor and richness to your everyday coffee.
