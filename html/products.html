Our Coffees

All our coffees are single-origin coffees, sourced from all over the world. The coffees are sent within two days from roasting so you consume fresh coffee. Just like any other fruit, each coffee has their own season. To always have the best in season, it's new coffee every month!

ERTHOPIAN YIRCHEFFE

A lighter roast brings out the exotic flavors of this Ethiopian Yirgacheffe. Many African coffees have unusual or exciting notes, but we especially wanted to showcase this selection’s unique mixture of citrusy lemon and floral flavors.

Medium Roast
Earl Grey Tea, Lemon, Floral
#Fruity

COSTA RICAN TARRAZY LA PASTORA

A classic, medium-roasted Costa Rican coffee, this Tarrazu will make an excellent cold or hot brew. With summer here, it is the perfect time of year for a versatile, refreshing coffee.

Medium Roast
Dark Chocolate, Almond, Caramel
#Classic

MEXICAN HUATUSCO ALTURA

We roasted this selection with our bold profile to enhance its body and bring out its natural sweetness. The darker roast accentuates the sweet cherry and brown sugar notes found in these beans.

Dark Roast
Cocoa, Cherry, Brown Sugar
#Bold

BRAZIL FAZENDA CALIFORNIA

We roasted this selecting with our bold profile, to enhance its body. The darker roast brings out the nutty flavors from the beans, such as hazelnut and almond butter notes.

Dark Roast
Dark Chocolate, Brown Sugar, Blueberry
#Bold

NICARAGUA MAMA MINA

We approached this coffee with our balanced profile, in order to showcase the beans’ origin characteristics. Enjoy the notes of milk chocolate, caramel and berries that are produced by Jinotega’s volcanic soil.

Light Rast
Milk Chcolate, Caramel, Berries
#Balanced

RWANDA DUKUNDE KAWA

Our fruity profile, which is a lighter roast, brings out the juicy, sweet and floral notes of this coffee.

Light Roast
Apricot, Jasmine, Black Tea
#Fruity

TANZANIA MBOZI

Our fruity profile, which is a lighter roast, brings out the honeydew, brown sugar and black tea notes of this coffee.

Light Roast
Honeydew, Brown Sugar, Black Tea
#Fruity

BRAZIL YELLOW CATUAI

Cautai is known for its high acidity, which we’ve tempered by roasting this lot with a classic profile. Our classic profile is a medium roast. It lets the coffee’s notes of dark chocolate, almond and caramel shine, while also enhancing the body.

Medium Roast
Dark Chocolate, Berries, Caramel
#Classic

EL SALVADOR SANTA MARIA

We roasted this selecting with our bold profile, to enhance its body. The darker roast brings out sweet flavors from the beans, such as caramel and dark chocolate notes.

Dark Roast
Dark Chocolate, Caramel, Cashew
#Bold

GUATEMALA LAS VOLCANES

Bringing back a classic Guatemalan coffee from either Antigua or Huehuetenango is always a treat. We’re excited to have this lot from Antigua that was grown by one of the most respected producers in the country. All we needed to do was roast this according to our balanced profile and let the producer’s work shine.

Medium Roast
Milk Chocolate, Orange, Caramel
#Balanced

ETHIOPIAN SIDAMO

We don’t often take African coffees into our Bold profile, but roasting this Ethiopian Sidamo slightly darker than usual brings out the roast while maintaining the beans’ freshness and acidity.

Dark Roast
Dark-Chocolate, Cherry, Strawberry
#Bold

BURUNDI BURAMIRO

As Burundi’s coffee season draws to a close, we’re excited to have one last lot that is perfect for a bright spring afternoon. With a light roast, this hand-sorted coffee’s clean finish is refreshing, the perfect complement for a warm, sunny day.

Light Roast
Floral, Cranberry Juice, Brown Sugar
#Fruity

MEXICO CANOAS

A classic-tasting coffee, this lot features characteristics that remind us of Costa Rica and Colombia. We decided to approach this selection with our classic roast profile, in order to show off it’s timeless taste

Medium Roast
Toasted Pine, Earthy, Raisins
#Classic

COLOMBIA OPORAPA

From the Western Huila region of Colombia, this coffee is difficult to find, so we’re being extra careful with it. Roasting it a touch more than our usual Balanced coffees showcases these beans beautifully.

Medium Roast
Red Grapes, Pecan, Milk Chocolate
#Balanced

COSTA RICA TARRAZU

A classic, medium-roasted Costa Rican coffee, this Tarrazu will make an excellent cold or hot brew. With spring here and summer quickly approaching, it is the perfect time of year for a versatile, refreshing coffee.

Medium Roast
Cocoa, Hazelnut, Brown Sugar
#Classic

ETHIOPIAN KOCHERE

A classic washed Ethiopian Yirgacheffe, this lot’s beans were small (which is common for coffees from Yirgacheffe). We used a little extra heat at the start of the roast, to bring out all of the small, dense beans’ flavor.

Light Roast
Black Tea, Floral, Lemongrass
#Fruity

HONDURAS SAN VICENTE

From a micro-climate rich with volcanic soil comes this unique Honduran coffee. This selection puts a twist on the classic coffee profile that Honduras is so well-known for. We’ve roasted these batches just a bit further, to bring out the roast flavor while maintaining the beans’ original richness.

Dark Roast
Cocoa, Pepper, Cherry
#Bold

UGANDA KAPCHORWA

Uganda Kapchorwa is both raw and sweet, Just like the country it comes from. Its bold roast captures the untamed beauty of Uganda, while an underlying sweetness permeates both the coffee and the country.

Dark Roast
Dates, Maple Syrup, Cocoa
#Bold

RWANDA GITESI

A Rwandan coffee that we loved last year, we roasted this light to bring out the milder spices without compromising the beans’ sweetness.

Light Roast
Cane Sugar, Citrus, Allspice
#Fruity

BURUNDI BUHORWA

Each year, this coffee from Burundi’s Buhorwa washing station improves. This year, it features sweet notes of honey and vanilla with bright hints of apple. By quickly bringing the bean up to a high temperature when roasting, we are able to accentuate this coffee’s natural sweetness.

Light Roast
Honey, Vanilla, Red Apple
#Fruity
